# Business Insights Report

This report supports a client-ready analytics deliverable and can be shown as part of a data analytics or data science internship portfolio.

## Summary Metrics
- Total sales: $2,297,200.86
- Total profit: $286,397.02
- Average discount: 15.62%
- Overall profit margin: 12.47%

## Key Findings
- Highest profit category: **Technology**
- Lowest profit category: **Furniture**
- Best region for profit: **West**
- Worst region for profit: **Central**
- Category with the lowest profit margin: **Furniture**

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing and inventory on high-margin categories and the highest-profit region.
- Re-evaluate discounting in low-margin categories, especially the one with the weakest margin.
- Review shipping practices for ship modes with the lowest profit contribution.
- Use the monthly sales/profit trend to identify slow months and plan promotional campaigns around them.
- Highlight top-performing products in sales campaigns and protect inventory levels for those items.
